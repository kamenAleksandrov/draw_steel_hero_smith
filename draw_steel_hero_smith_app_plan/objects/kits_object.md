[[kits_creator]]
each kit has all of or a combination of:

- armor categories
- weapon categories
- kit bonuses (to any of the hero stats) per echelon if the hero has more than 1 kit, only the highest bonuses apply
	- stamina bonus
	- speed bonus
	- stability bonus
	- mele damage bonus
	- ranged damage bonus
	- mele distance bonus
	- ranged distance bonus
	- disengage bonus
- ability
- feature
- descriptive text
